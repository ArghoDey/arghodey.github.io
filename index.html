About

I am a Master's student in Computer Technology at the College of Computer Science, Chongqing University, advised by Prof. Yunfei Yin. I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Technology at Jiangxi University of Technology, graduating first in my class.

My research centers on perception and decision-making for autonomous driving — in particular Bird's-Eye-View (BEV) scene understanding, multimodal camera–radar fusion, and lightweight attention mechanisms that run reliably on resource-constrained hardware. More broadly I work across deep learning, computer vision, and artificial intelligence.
